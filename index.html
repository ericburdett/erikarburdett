What is Growth Mindset?

A growth mindset is the belief that our abilities and intelligence can be developed through dedication, hard work, and perseverance. Unlike a fixed mindset, which assumes our qualities are carved in stone, a growth mindset embraces challenges, persists in the face of setbacks, sees effort as the path to mastery, learns from criticism, and finds lessons and inspiration in the success of others.

Through this platform, we'll explore practical strategies and insights to cultivate a growth mindset in your personal and professional life, leading to greater achievement and fulfillment.
